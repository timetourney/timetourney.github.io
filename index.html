Who We Are

Welcome to the electrifying world of The Igiehon Mathematics Event, where numbers come alive and equations dance! We lead the way in organizing exhilarating mathematics competitions for high school students in Edo State, Nigeria, with aspirations to expand this initiative nationwide.

Embark on an thrilling journey packed with brain-teasing challenges, intense competitions, and boundless chances to exhibit your mathematical brilliance.

At TIME, we believe that math is not just a subject; it's an exicting adventure waiting to be explored. Are you ready to dive into the world of Math?
